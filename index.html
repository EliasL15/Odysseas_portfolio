About Me

Odysseas is currently studying Film Music BMus at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama. Coming from a background as a guitarist, he has performed in numerous rock and metal bands, recording an album with the band Gas Station Lollipops and performing in various venues around Nicosia, Cyprus. After moving to London, he has started his career as a film composer and sound designer. He has arranged for OrchestRAVE, an orchestral remix which will be uploaded soon on YouTube, orchestrated and sound designed for The Road Ahead short film series which will be screened at the Barbican Cinema, and collaborated with animators from Central Saint Martins as a composer and a sound designer. He has also written a piece which was performed at the National Gallery by a string quartet, inspired by a painting by Turner, as well as creating soundscapes for a live theatre experience collaboration with LIFT.
